### Important Legal Principles (Thai Context)

- Contracts must appear fair to both parties for Thai court enforceability
- Thai Land Office has specific requirements for lease registration
- One-sided contracts risk being deemed unenforceable
- Professional legal drafting should reflect balance even when protecting the lessee
